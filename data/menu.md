### Crispy Calamari

- Price: $12
- Vegan: No (contains seafood, egg)

### Garden Soup
Seasonal vegetable soup simmered in a herb-infused vegetable broth. Served with crusty bread.

## Main Courses

### Grilled Atlantic Salmon
Fresh Atlantic salmon fillet, grilled with lemon-butter sauce, served with roasted asparagus and garlic mashed potatoes.
- Price: $26
- Vegan: No (contains fish, dairy)
- Gluten-Free: Yes
- Allergens: Fish, Dairy
- Cooking method: Grilled

### Pasta Primavera (Vegan)
Penne pasta tossed with seasonal vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, spinach) in a light garlic-olive oil sauce. No cream, no cheese.
- Price: $18
- Vegan: Yes
- Gluten-Free: No (contains gluten)
- Allergens: Gluten

### Spaghetti Carbonara
Classic spaghetti with creamy egg-based sauce, pancetta, parmesan, and black pepper.
- Price: $20
- Vegan: No (contains egg, dairy, pork)
- Gluten-Free: No
- Allergens: Gluten, Egg, Dairy

### Truffle Mushroom Risotto
Arborio rice slow-cooked with wild mushrooms, truffle oil, and parmesan. Topped with fresh thyme.
- Price: $22
- Vegan: No (contains dairy — parmesan)
- Gluten-Free: Yes
- Allergens: Dairy
- Note: Can be made vegan by omitting parmesan on request.

### Grilled Chicken Breast
Free-range chicken breast, herb-marinated and grilled. Served with roasted potatoes and seasonal salad.
- Price: $21
- Vegan: No (contains chicken)
- Gluten-Free: Yes
- Allergens: None
- Cooking method: Grilled (not fried)

### Beef Tenderloin Steak
200g prime beef tenderloin, cooked to your preferred doneness. Served with fries and peppercorn sauce.
- Price: $38
- Vegan: No
- Gluten-Free: Yes (fries cooked in dedicated fryer)
- Allergens: Dairy (sauce)
- Cooking method: Grilled

### Falafel Wrap (Vegan)
House-made falafel in a warm flatbread with hummus, tabbouleh, pickled vegetables, and tahini sauce.
- Price: $15
- Vegan: Yes
