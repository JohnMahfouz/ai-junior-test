# NovaBite Restaurant — Policies & Information

---

## Opening Hours

### Downtown Branch
- Monday – Thursday: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- Friday: 11:00 AM – 11:30 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM – 11:30 PM
- Sunday: 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM
- Kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time.

### Uptown Branch
- Monday – Friday: 12:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Saturday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM
- Sunday: 11:00 AM – 9:00 PM
- Kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time.

### Airport Branch
- Open 7 days a week: 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM
- No reservations required at the Airport branch.

---

## Table Reservations

- Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance.
- We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes past the booking time.
- For groups of 8 or more, please call the branch directly.
- Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before the reservation time.
- No-show fee: $15 per person for groups of 5 or more.

---

## Refund & Cancellation Policy

- Full refund for cancellations made more than 24 hours before a dining event or catering booking.
- 50% refund for cancellations between 2 and 24 hours before the event.
- No refund for cancellations less than 2 hours before the event.
- Food quality complaints must be raised with the manager on duty during the visit. Refunds or replacements will be offered at the manager's discretion.
- Delivery orders: Report issues within 1 hour of delivery for a refund or replacement.

---

## Loyalty Program — NovaBite Rewards

- Every $1 spent earns 10 loyalty points.
- Points can be redeemed as follows:
  - 500 points = $5 discount
  - 1000 points = $12 discount
  - 2000 points = $25 discount
  - 5000 points = Free meal up to $40 value
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.
- Points cannot be earned on already-discounted items or promotions.
- Sign up at any branch or on the NovaBite app.
- Loyalty points are linked to your registered phone number or email.

## Private Events & Catering

### In-Restaurant Private Events
- NovaBite Downtown has a private dining room that fits up to 25 guests.
- NovaBite Uptown has a semi-private area for up to 40 guests.
- Minimum spend applies: $500 for Downtown private room, $800 for Uptown semi-private area.
- Events must be booked at least 5 days in advance.
- Birthday events: Complimentary cake cutting service and anniversary decoration setup available upon request.

### Catering Packages

#### Standard Catering Package
- Minimum 10 guests, maximum 100 guests.
- Includes: 2 starters, 2 main courses, 1 dessert, non-alcoholic beverages.
- Price: $35 per person.

#### Premium Catering Package
- Minimum 20 guests, maximum 200 guests.
- Includes: 3 starters, 3 main courses, 2 desserts, full beverage package (alcoholic + non-alcoholic), dedicated event coordinator, table linen and centerpiece setup.
- Price: $65 per person.

#### Corporate Catering Package
- For office lunches, meetings, and corporate events.
- Minimum 15 guests.
- Includes: Sandwich platters, salads, wraps, fruit platters, coffee and tea service.
- Price: $25 per person.
- Available Monday to Friday, 11 AM – 4 PM.

### Catering Booking Requirements
- 50% deposit required at booking.
- Final guest count must be confirmed 48 hours before the event.
- NovaBite provides delivery and setup within a 20km radius of each branch.

---

## Dietary & Allergy Policy

- NovaBite takes allergies seriously. Please inform your server of any allergies before ordering.
- While we take precautions, our kitchen handles gluten, nuts, dairy, egg, and shellfish. Cross-contamination is possible.
- Vegan dishes are prepared with separate utensils upon request.
- Gluten-free pasta and bread are available as substitutes (additional charge may apply).

---

## Children's Policy

- Children are welcome at all branches.
- High chairs and booster seats are available at no extra charge.
- Children's menu available for guests under 12.
- Children's menu items are priced at $10–$14 and include a kids' drink.

---

## Pet Policy

- Well-behaved dogs are welcome in outdoor seating areas only.
- No pets allowed in indoor dining areas (health regulations).
